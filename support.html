How do I extract places?

Tap the + button in the app, or share a link from Instagram or TikTok directly to Somedays using the share sheet. The app pulls out every place mentioned and saves them to a collection.

How do I manage my subscription?

Go to Settings in the app and tap "Manage Subscription". You can also manage it from your iPhone's Settings > Apple ID > Subscriptions.

How do I restore my purchase?

Open the upgrade screen and tap "Restore Purchases" at the bottom. This works if you previously purchased on another device or reinstalled the app.

How do I delete my account?

Go to Settings in the app and tap "Delete Account". This removes all your data from our servers. You can export your data as JSON first.

The extraction didn't find any places

Somedays works best with Instagram Reels and TikTok videos that mention specific places — restaurants, shops, hotels, and more. If the video doesn't name specific locations, the extraction may not find results.
